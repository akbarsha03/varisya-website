Questions

The details.

Where does the software run?

On your own infrastructure — on-premise or your cloud account. Varisya is the technology partner; you hold the servers, the data and the access. Hosting is yours to provision (we'll advise), and never billed through us.

What exactly am I paying for monthly?

Two things, bundled: a support SLA and a defined amount of ongoing customization capacity (dev-days). It's a retainer for a dedicated team that keeps shaping the system to your business — not a per-seat licence.

What's not included?

Anything metered by a third party: hosting/infrastructure, SMS, WhatsApp Business API, payment-gateway fees and external API usage. These stay on your own accounts and are billed at cost — no markup, full transparency.

How is this different from 399apps?

399apps is shared cloud, self-serve, priced for volume. Varisya is the opposite end: bespoke engineering, on your own infrastructure, white-labelled, with a dedicated team and SLA. Same product DNA — a fundamentally different level of service.

Can you migrate our existing data?

Yes. Onboarding and data migration are included in every engagement, with a staging environment so you validate before anything goes live.
